About Me

Hello! I'm Alex Chen, a passionate photographer with over 8 years of experience capturing life's most precious moments. My journey began with a simple love for storytelling through images, and has evolved into a career dedicated to preserving memories that last a lifetime.

I specialize in wedding, portrait, and event photography, but my true passion lies in connecting with people and understanding their unique stories. Every photo session is an opportunity to create something beautiful and meaningful.

When I'm not behind the camera, you can find me exploring new locations, studying light and composition, or spending time with my family. I believe that great photography comes from genuine connections and authentic moments.
