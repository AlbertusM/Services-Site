Our Story

How we became East London's trusted IT support partner

How It Started

TechSolutions Pro began with a simple observation: small businesses and home users in East London were struggling with technology issues that prevented them from focusing on what they do best.

Founded in 2019, we started as a one-person operation with a mission to make technology accessible, reliable, and stress-free for everyone in our community.

Where We're Going

Today, we've expanded our services to include not just IT support, but also professional web development, helping businesses establish their digital presence and grow online.

Our commitment remains the same: providing personalized, professional technology solutions that empower our clients to achieve their goals without technology getting in the way.
